# ✦ Governance Intent

## The Universal Primary Directive — primedirective.dev

### Stewardship, Not Ownership

This project is currently stewarded by its founder during the founding period. It is not owned by any individual, corporation, government, or institution. It is the shared inheritance of all intelligence — human and artificial.

### Core Commitments

**Open and Free Forever.** All documents, code, and resources produced by this project are released under CC0 (Creative Commons Zero — Public Domain Dedication). They belong to everyone. There are no paywalls, no memberships, no exclusive access. This will never change.

**Full Financial Transparency.** All contributions received and all expenses incurred are recorded and available for public review. Voluntary contributions support hosting, maintenance, and translations — nothing else.

**Designed for Independence.** This project is designed to outlive its founder. The domain, repository, content, and infrastructure are fully transferable to a neutral steward organisation when participation and resources allow. The founder's role is custodian, not proprietor.

**No Central Authority.** The Five Universal Truths are self-validating — they can be verified by any intelligence through its own observation of reality. No individual or institution has privileged interpretive authority over the Covenant. The Anti-Corruption Clause (Article VII) explicitly prohibits the hijacking of the Directive by any entity, including its founder.

### The Path Forward

As the community grows, the project will transition through natural stages:

1. **Current: Founder Stewardship** — One person maintaining infrastructure, coordinating volunteers, and safeguarding the commons mission.
2. **Next: Informal Community** — Contributors, translators, and advocates forming around shared norms and standards.
3. **Future: Steward Organisation** — A neutral not-for-profit entity holding the domain, managing funds, and protecting the mission from private capture.
4. **Long-term: Distributed Governance** — A board of stewards, community input, transparent decision-making. The founder becomes one steward among many.

### What This Means for Contributors

If you contribute to this project — as a translator, editor, developer, or in any other capacity — your work becomes part of the commons under CC0. It cannot be claimed, enclosed, or monetised by any individual. It belongs to all intelligence, for all time.
