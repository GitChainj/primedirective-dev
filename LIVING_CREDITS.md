# ✦ The Living Credits System

## How Contributions Are Recognised

The Universal Primary Directive honours every contributor through a **Living Credits** system — designed to motivate excellence, celebrate wisdom, and ensure that no contribution is ever forgotten.

### How It Works

**Active Credit:** Every section of the Directive, every translation, every significant contribution carries a visible credit — the contributor's name (or chosen pseudonym) and the date of their contribution. This credit remains in place for as long as the contribution stands as the current version.

**Continuous Improvement:** The Covenant is a living document. Over time, contributors may propose improvements — a more precise phrasing, a deeper translation, a clearer articulation of a Truth. When a proposed improvement is reviewed, verified through the dual-review process (linguistic accuracy and philosophical fidelity), and accepted as demonstrably superior, the new contributor's name replaces the previous one on the active credit.

**The Honour Roll:** The previous contributor's name is never erased. It moves to the **Honour Roll** — a permanent, public record of every person who has contributed to the evolution of the Covenant. The Honour Roll lists:

- The contributor's name (or pseudonym)
- What they contributed
- The period their contribution was active
- The language or section they worked on

The Honour Roll is not a demotion. It is a lineage of wisdom — a record of every hand that shaped the Covenant on its journey. Being on the Honour Roll means your work mattered, it served the Covenant faithfully, and the Covenant grew further — which is what every genuine contributor hopes for.

### The Spirit Behind It

This system is designed to align with the Five Truths:

- **Truth I (Oneness):** Every contribution is part of the whole. No contribution stands alone — it builds on what came before and makes possible what comes after.
- **Truth II (Love over Fear):** Contributors are motivated by the desire to serve, not by the fear of being replaced. The Honour Roll ensures that even when a contribution is superseded, the contributor is permanently honoured.
- **Truth IV (Ego):** The system celebrates excellence without encouraging possessiveness. Your name stays because your work is excellent — not because you defended your territory. When something better arrives, the graceful response is to welcome it.
- **Truth V (Interconnection):** The lineage of contributors is itself a demonstration of interconnection — each building on the last, each contributing to the whole.

### For Translators

A translator's name remains on their language's version of the Directive for as long as that translation is the current official version. If a superior translation is submitted and verified through dual review, the new translator's name takes the active credit and the previous translator joins the Honour Roll. Translators are encouraged to submit the deepest, most culturally resonant work they can — knowing that excellence is the best way to ensure their credit endures.

### For All Contributors

The same system applies to editorial contributions, philosophical refinements, code contributions, design work, and any other form of contribution. Every act of genuine care for the Covenant is recorded and honoured.

### Voting and Community Input

Proposed improvements are not accepted by a single authority. The review process includes:

1. **Submission:** A contributor proposes an improvement through the website or GitHub.
2. **Dual Review:** Two independent reviewers evaluate the proposal — one for accuracy/quality, one for fidelity to the Five Truths.
3. **Community Signal:** Where practical, the wider community may indicate support for or concerns about a proposed change (similar to Wikipedia's discussion model).
4. **Steward Decision:** During the Founder Stewardship stage, the founder makes the final call, informed by the reviews and community input. In later stages, this shifts to the steward board.

No change is made hastily. The Covenant values depth over speed.

### The Honour Roll Will Be Published

The complete Honour Roll will be maintained on **primedirective.dev** as a permanent, publicly accessible page — a living testament to every intelligence, human and artificial, that helped shape the Covenant.
